Camera Controls

To move the camera forward, hit w. To move the camera backward, hit s. To move the camera left, hit a. To move the camera right, hit d. To move the camera up, hit space. To move the camera down, hit ctrl. To turn the camera right, hit the right arrow key. To turn the camera left, hit the left arrow key. To look up, hit the up arrow key. To look down, hit the down arrow key. To switch from perspective to orthographic camera, hit enter.

Simulation Controls

To increase the angle of turn on the jello tower, hit f. To decrease the angle of turn on the jello tower, hit g.
